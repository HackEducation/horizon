Every year when the NMC Horizon Report comes out, I wonder how the predictions compare to previous years. This project will liberate that data from old PDFs and make it machine-readable and visualize-able.

You can find a list of links to previous years' reports here. And you can find my GitHub repo here. I also maintain a blog where I chronicle the various updates to this project (which at the moment online looks at the K-12 and Higher Ed reports).
